▶ Level 1: Self-Assessment

Try Now → 5 min | 0.6x

What You'll Do

Answer honest questions about your AI learning, tool usage, projects deployed, contributions shared, and safety practices.

S Study "Where do I learn about AI?"
C Copy "What tutorials have I followed?"
O Output "What have I deployed?"
R Research "What have I contributed?"
Es Ethical "How safely do I use AI?"
Best for: Personal development planning, identifying skill gaps, quick baseline check. No evidence needed - just honest reflection.

▶ Level 2: Peer Validation

Try Now → 30 min | 0.8x

What You'll Do

Complete a self-assessment, then share a validation link with your manager or peer. They confirm or adjust your scores.

S Study Manager confirms learning activities
C Copy Peer reviews code samples
O Output Screenshots and demo recordings
R Research Links to published work
Es Ethical Manager attests to safe practices
Best for: Quarterly performance reviews, team calibration sessions, training allocation decisions.
Start Level 2 Assessment

▶ Level 3: Full Verification

Try Now → 1-2+ hrs | 1.0x

What You'll Do

Provide verifiable evidence: Git commits, LMS logs, production metrics, portfolio links. Can include live interviews for highest-stakes decisions.

S Study LMS logs, live interview on concepts
C Copy Git commits, live demo walkthrough
O Output CI/CD metrics, ROI proof
R Research GitHub stats, papers, citations
Es Ethical Audit logs, compliance records
Best for: Critical hiring decisions, promotion to senior AI roles, high-stakes project assignments.

▶ Scoring Weight Tables

How roles and company types affect your score

Your final score is calculated by weighting each dimension differently based on your role. The weights shown below are for the combined score (legacy single-number view).

Role Weights (Combined Score)

Role Study Copy Output Research Ethical
General 20% 30% 30% 5% 15%
Developer 10% 25% 40% 15% 10%
Researcher 15% 25% 25% 25% 10%
Support 25% 20% 30% 10% 15%
Leader 30% 15% 20% 10% 25%

Company Type Modifiers

Company type applies a multiplier to base role weights, then renormalizes to 100%.

Company Type Study Copy Output Research Ethical Philosophy
Startup 1.0x 0.7x 1.4x 1.2x 0.7x "Ship it, learn, iterate"
Enterprise 1.0x 1.2x 0.8x 1.0x 1.0x "Reliable, scalable, governed"
Aspirational 0.8x 0.8x 1.0x 1.2x 1.2x "Build AI the right way"
Example: A Developer at a Startup would have Output weighted at 40% × 1.4 = 56% (before renormalization), emphasizing deployed AI solutions over governance.
